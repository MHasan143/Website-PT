Our Products

Discover high‑quality installation materials designed to ensure flawless, long‑lasting tile work. From adhesives to additives and grouts, Prime Tiles offers reliable products of STILE Pakistan that meet the needs of homeowners, contractors, and professionals alike.

Bond Thin‑Set Mortar – 20 KG (Grey)

STILE Bond is a high-performance cementitious mortar designed for the interior and exterior installation of all kinds of tiles and natural stone on walls & floors. It offers a bond that is five times stronger than ordinary Portland cement, providing high-tech sticking that holds tiles securely without slippage.

LATEX 73 – 1 Litre

Available at Prime Tiles, an authorized dealer of Stile Pakistan, STILE Latex 73 is a liquid admixture that enhances the performance of any kind of cement mortar, including tile bond. Formulated with advanced polymers & additives, it offers superior performance in various tiling projects, ensuring reliable results.

Grout Admix – 1 Litre

STILE Grout Admix is a liquid polymer admixture formulated for use with STILE Unsanded Grout to enhance the efficiency & durability of unsanded grout. It reduces problems like cracking, pinholes, color fading & shrinkage.

Stain‑Resistant Grout – 1kg

Being an authorized dealer of STILE Pakistan, Prime Tiles is proud to offer Pakistan’s first-ever Stain-Resistant Grout that complies by European Quality Standards. It offers enhanced resistance to stains and water for preserving beauty of your spaces for a longer time! Designed to uplift & enhance the aesthetic feel of any living space, it has been specifically engineered to be durable, long-lasting, resistant against cracks or shrinkage.

Unsanded Grout

STILE Unsanded grout is a blend of Portland cement, chemical additives, and colour fast pigments. Available in 21 colours, it complements the appearance of any wall or floor tile. It is water-resistant, crack-resistant, and enhances the performance of STILE Grout Admix.

Epoxy Tile Grout

STILE Epoxy Tile Grout is a premium two-component grout that offers exceptional durability, chemical resistance, and long-lasting color stability. With its smooth, stain-free, and waterproof finish, it ensures easy maintenance & lasting elegance, making it ideal for aesthetic, decorative, and high-performance tile joint finishing in residential, commercial, & industrial spaces.
