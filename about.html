About Us

Prime Tiles is a trusted tile dealership based in Karachi, Pakistan, established in 2018, proudly offering premium products from some of the most reputable and well‑known tile manufacturers in the industry. As an authorized dealer, we bring you a diverse selection of high‑quality ceramic, porcelain, and vitrified tiles, along with decorative options sourced from leading brands in the country.

Our mission is to make top‑tier tile designs accessible to homeowners, architects, and builders across Pakistan. With a focus on authenticity, durability, and modern aesthetics, we ensure that every product we offer meets the highest standards of quality.

At Prime Tiles, we believe in guiding our customers with honesty and expertise. Whether you're renovating a home or completing a commercial project, we help you choose the right tiles to match your vision and budget — backed by reliable service and genuine products from trusted manufacturers.
