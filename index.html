Our Manufacturers

At Prime Tiles, we proudly collaborate with some of the most reputable, innovative, and globally recognized tile manufacturers to bring our customers premium-quality tiles for every residential and commercial project. Our trusted supplier network includes industry leaders such as Shabbir Tiles & Ceramics, Ocean Ceramics Pvt. Showcasing our suppliers allows us to provide complete transparency and confidence to our customers. Every tile at Prime Tiles is backed by proven quality, innovative design, and the manufacturing excellence of our partners—ensuring that your spaces are built with materials you can trust.
